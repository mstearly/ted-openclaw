# JC-040 - Guided Intake Wizard

## Outcome

Turn intake into a guided experience that captures intent in plain English and outputs a safe starter job card with KPI suggestions.

## Non-negotiables

- Each step explains why requested input matters.
- AI recommendations stay within governance constraints.

- AI-generated starter card with hard bans and KPI pack.
- Save path with audit trace.

## Friction KPI Evidence

- intake completion rate
- time to first usable job card
- post-intake correction rate
